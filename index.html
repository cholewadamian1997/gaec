Whispers of Winter:
A Christmas Musical Journey

Celebrate with music that inspires and unites!

Date: December 19, 2024 | Time: 16:00 and 18:30

Venue: Yehudi Menuhin Forum, Helvetiapl. 6, 3005 Bern

About the Event

We invite you to a magical New Year's concert organized by the Global Artistry and Enterprise Club (GAEC). This unique event brings together musical traditions from around the world, performed by the professional ensemble "Musikschatulle."

Program & Apero

Program

  • International New Year's Songs
  • Classical Music
  • Jazz Compositions
  • Famous Film Melodies

The repertoire includes musical pieces from different corners of the world, creating a festive mood and offering inspiration.

Apero

Between concerts, we invite you to an apero, where a signature wine collection from Moldova will be presented, along with light snacks and drinks in a cozy atmosphere.

Payment: Payment for the apero can be made on-site in cash or via Twint.

Multilingual Support

The concert will be conducted in English, with translation available into German, Russian, and Tatar.
